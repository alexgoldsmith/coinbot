About Electroneum

Electroneum (ETN) is a cryptocurrency that launched in 2017 with its own blockchain developed on the explicit intention of gaining mass adoption by targeting mobile audiences and smart phone users. Offering to users dedicated mobile mining applications available in many languages to anyone with a typical handheld smart device, this project has shown an effort to make it so those who wish to become involved in the revolutionary technology may do so with relative ease and see results in the form of small amounts of ETN each day for their participation. With the idea that access to the technology underpinning Bitcoin could be made simpler for todays global economies, the teams behind Elecroneum genuinely aim to make their blockchain and cryptocurrency readily available to the ordinary mobile user and to spur rapid viral growth extending credit to their actively engaged community.
