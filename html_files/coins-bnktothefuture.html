About BnkToTheFuture

BnKToTheFuture (BFT) is an online investment platform created to facilitate investments in equity and cryptoassets. The BFT ERC-20 token functions as a membership and rewards mechanism to create an efficient and transparent marketplace on BnKToTheFuture.com.

Those with at least 10,000 BFTs are able to gain priority access to syndicated deals on the platform, which included the likes of Telegrams TON.

The project is led by Simon Dixon, who was an early investor in the likes of BitStamp, Kraken, Bitfinex, ShapeShift, Bitpay, Kim, BitPesa, Unocoin, MaidSafe, Coinsecure, Factom and Uphold.
